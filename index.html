10 Mill St.

  The new owner of the lovely building located in 10 mill street is a young Archit...ect and Designer from Brazil. He is interested in creating a boutique hotel in the Liberties after he fell in love with this building and the history of the surrounding area, such as the free spirit of the area combined with the whiskey brewing history of the Golden Triangle.

In Aditional to the history of the area, another key factor for it was the quiet city.

The initial idea for the hotel would be to create a sophisticated man cave where people can go to relax, meet people, and feel safe from the outer world.


Chocolate Factory

  This was an individual project and involved exploring the potential re-use of existing buildings to a Multi-Discip...linary Design Studio.

The client is a community of designers, artists, and small enterprises from different disciplines that wish to work together in a creative and stimulating environment. They include an interior designer, a graphic designer, furniture designer, filmmaker, photographer, event planner, jeweler, fine artist, chocolatier, and web designer.

They are excited by the idea of sharing ideas and collaborating on projects together. They want the building they work in to impress their clients as soon as they enter the front door. They have seen similar projects in London, Stockholm, Berlin, New York and they would like to carefully examine innovative office design.


Red Stables

  The client, a Development Company that has acquired a rural building, The Red Stables, wants to transform it into a comfortable and welco...ming space for all prople. The client wishes to adapt a historic building to a residential apartment house complex for people of all ages. The apartments should be a mix of sizes going from studios to three bedrooms apartment. The client wants the architect to be conscious of the historical value of the building so as to avoid unnecessary damage to the original envelope. All extensions should be easily removable.

The client wishes that the construction of the new apartments have a low carbon footprint and with the use of smart technologies to turn the structure into a near passive housing. Also to be included renewable sources of energy and waste treatments.
