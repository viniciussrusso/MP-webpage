10 Mill St.


  The new owner of the lovely building located in 10 mill street is a young Architect and Designer from Brazil. He is interested in creating a boutique hotel in the Liberties after he fell in love with this building and the history of the surrounding area, such as the free spirit of the area combined with the whiskey brewing history of the Golden Triangle.

  The initial idea for the hotel would be to create a sophisticated man cave where people can go to relax, meet people, and feel safe from the outer world

  In Aditional to the history of the area, another key factor for it was the quiet location but with a still good connection to the surrounding areas of the city.

  Recently same-sex marriage began legally in Ireland, so the new space is to welcome all kinds of guests and visitors in this safe environment. They can enjoy the bar amenities and the new greenhouse restaurant to be placed in the back of the building connecting the hotel guests with the nearby accommodations and office people.
